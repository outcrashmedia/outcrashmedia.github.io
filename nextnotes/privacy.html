Privacy Policy

Your notes stay yours — always

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to NextNotes ("we", "us" or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and the security of your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

By using NextNotes you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

🔒 Important: NextNotes stores NO data on our servers

NextNotes is a privacy-first app that keeps your notes under your control:

  • ✓ No own servers: We do NOT operate any servers where your notes or credentials are stored
  • ✓ Local-first: All notes are stored in a local database on your device
  • ✓ Your Nextcloud: The app only connects to your own Nextcloud server — configured by you
  • ✓ No note analytics: We never read, store, or analyze your note content
  • ✓ Only 2 third parties: Google AdMob (ads) and RevenueCat (subscriptions)

This Privacy Policy covers only these two third-party services. Everything else stays entirely on your device and your Nextcloud server.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Nextcloud server credentials: When you connect to your Nextcloud server, you provide your server URL, username, and an app password. These credentials are stored locally on your device using the secure OS Keychain (iOS) or Keystore (Android) — never in plain text and never sent to us.
  • Notes and content: Notes you create or edit are stored locally in a SQLite database on your device and synced only to your Nextcloud server when you choose.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, and anonymous device identifiers collected by third-party SDKs (see Section 3).
  • Usage data: Anonymized information about how you interact with app features, collected by third-party SDKs.

3. Third-Party Services

NextNotes integrates the following third-party services, which may collect information that could be used to identify you:

Google AdMob

Purpose: Displaying advertisements in the free version of the App.

Data collected: Device identifiers, IP address, location data, usage data, and advertising IDs.

Privacy policy: Google AdMob Privacy Policy

Note: Ads are disabled for NextNotes Pro subscribers.

RevenueCat

Purpose: Managing in-app subscriptions for NextNotes Pro.

Data collected: Purchase history, subscription status, and anonymous user IDs.

Privacy policy: RevenueCat Privacy Policy

Data storage: RevenueCat stores purchase receipts and subscription information to manage your subscription status across devices.

Your Nextcloud Server

Purpose: Syncing your notes (configured and controlled entirely by you).

Data involved: Notes you create, edit, or delete are synced to the Nextcloud server you configure.

Privacy note: We do not operate your Nextcloud server. Data stored there is subject to the privacy policy and security configuration of your own server. Your Nextcloud app password is stored only in your device's secure enclave and is never transmitted to us.

More info: Nextcloud Privacy Information

4. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing and maintaining the App's functionality
  • Syncing your notes with your Nextcloud server
  • Managing your NextNotes Pro subscription status
  • Displaying relevant advertising (free version only)
  • Improving and optimizing App performance
  • Providing customer support
  • Complying with legal obligations

5. Data Storage and Security

Local-first storage: All notes and credentials are stored locally on your device. We never have access to your note content.

Security Measures

  • Secure storage: Nextcloud app passwords are stored using platform-native secure mechanisms — Keychain on iOS, Keystore on Android.
  • Local database: Notes are stored in a local SQLite database. No note content is sent to us.
  • HTTPS: All connections to your Nextcloud server use HTTPS encryption.
  • No cloud backup to us: We do not back up your notes or credentials to our own servers — because we don't have any.

While we implement appropriate security measures, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share information.
  • Service providers: With the third-party services listed in Section 3 to deliver App functionality.
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority.
  • Business transfers: In connection with mergers, sales of company assets, or acquisitions.

7. Your Data Protection Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access: Request access to your personal data.
  • Right to rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your personal data by uninstalling the App and removing your local notes.
  • Data portability: Export your notes at any time by accessing your Nextcloud server directly.
  • Right to object: Object to the processing of your personal data.
  • Right to restriction: Request restriction of processing of your data.
  • Withdrawal of consent: Withdraw consent at any time (e.g. by revoking the Nextcloud app password in your Nextcloud settings).

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@nextnotes.app.

8. Children's Privacy

NextNotes is not intended for use by children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete such information.

9. Advertising and Opt-Out

The free version of NextNotes displays ads via Google AdMob. These ads may use cookies and similar technologies to serve relevant advertisements based on your interests. You can opt out of personalized advertising by:

  • On iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → Turn off Personalized Ads
  • On Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID
  • By upgrading to NextNotes Pro, which disables all ads

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, through an in-app notification. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

11. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: privacy@nextnotes.app

We will respond within 30 days.
