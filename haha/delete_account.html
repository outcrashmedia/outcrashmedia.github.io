Delete Account

As a user of the Haha App, you have the right to request the deletion of your account and associated personal data. We have established a simple process to help you with this.

How to delete your account:

  1. Send an email to outcrashmedia@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line "Haha App - Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include your registered email address in the email
Send Email

What data will be deleted?

After confirming your request, the following data will be deleted:

  • Your user account and profile information
  • Jokes created and shared by you
  • Your activity data (likes, comments, etc.)
  • Your saved preferences

Data Retention

For legal reasons and service improvement purposes, certain data may be retained for a limited period:

  • Anonymized usage statistics: Permanently retained for analytical purposes
  • Legally required information: Retained according to legal requirements (typically 6 years)
  • Security-relevant logs: Retained for 30 days
