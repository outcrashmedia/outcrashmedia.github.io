Child Protection Standards

1. Our Commitment to Child Safety

At Haha, we maintain a zero-tolerance policy regarding sexual abuse and exploitation of children. We are committed to providing a safe environment for all users and take specific measures to protect children from harmful content and exploitation.

2. Content Moderation and Reporting

  • In-App Reporting: Every joke in our app has a report button for immediate flagging of inappropriate content. This allows users to quickly report jokes they find offensive or harmful.
  • Review Process: Our team reviews all reported content promptly, with priority given to reports involving child safety concerns. We have a dedicated team that carefully examines each report.
  • Immediate Action: Content found to violate our standards is immediately removed from the platform. We take swift action to remove any harmful material.
  • Multiple Reports: Jokes receiving multiple reports are reviewed more thoroughly and may be removed even if they don't initially appear to violate our guidelines.

3. Account Safety Measures

  • Account Bans: Users who violate our child protection policies face immediate account termination. We have a strict policy against those who endanger children.
  • Prevention of Re-registration: We implement measures to prevent banned users from creating new accounts. We actively work to prevent repeat offenders.

4. Law Enforcement Cooperation

We cooperate fully with law enforcement agencies in cases involving child exploitation and will report illegal content to relevant authorities as required by law. We take our legal obligations seriously.

5. Age Restrictions

Haha is intended for users 16 years of age or older. Users under 16 are prohibited from using the app.

6. Contact Us

If you have any concerns about child safety on Haha, please contact us immediately at outcrashmedia@gmail.com.
